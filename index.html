About Me

Hello, I'm Zekarias, a student at Augustana College with an ambitious vision and a unique blend of interests, majoring in Biochemistry and Computer Science. My academic journey allows me to explore the profound intersections of life sciences and cutting-edge technology, unraveling the possibilities that emerge when biology meets computing power. My passion is rooted in the transformative potential of technology within healthcare. Driven by a curiosity about how we can revolutionize patient care, I am especially drawn to the ways Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning can redefine what’s possible in medicine. I’m captivated by the idea that algorithms, when crafted with insight and precision, can help decode complex biological data, accelerating drug discovery, and bringing us closer to an era of truly personalized medicine. With my background in biochemistry, I understand the molecular foundations of life, and with my computational skills, I envision practical ways to address real-world health challenges. I’m inspired by the thought that I could contribute to discoveries that extend beyond laboratory walls to impact lives globally. As I look forward, my commitment is clear: to pioneer at the crossroads of Biochemistry, Computer Science, and healthcare, advancing solutions that push boundaries and harness the power of technology to improve human health and well-being.
